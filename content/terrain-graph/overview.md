# Shape worlds, not heightmaps

Terrain Graph is a node-based workflow for building repeatable landscapes while keeping artistic control close at hand.

## The workflow

- describe large forms with generators
- combine and mask regions
- preview results at multiple scales
- bake only when the world is ready
